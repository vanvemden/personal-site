About

I was born and raised in the Netherlands. The summer of 1996 I was offered an job at the Consulate General of the Netherlands in Los Angeles. It was a dream come true for my wife and I, so we packed up and moved to California. In 2010 the office office re-located to San Francisco, and that is how I ended up living in the East Bay. Still happily married, and proud father of two teenage California girls.

After 23 years in the Dutch diplomatic service, I decided it was time for a career change. Since the age of 15 I have been a passionate programmer, and now the time had come to become a Sofware Engineer. So, in May 2020 I started the 16 week immersive Full-Stack Web Developer bootcamp at Rithm School in San Francisco to 'brush up' on coding skills, problem solving techniques, and computer science knowledge.
