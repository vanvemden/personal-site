About

I was born and raised in the Netherlands. In the summer of 1996, I was offered a job at the Consulate General of the Netherlands in Los Angeles. It was a dream come true for my wife and I, so we packed up and moved to Redondo Beach, California. In 2010 the consulate re-located to San Francisco, and we live in the East Bay now. Still happily married, and proud father of two teenage California girls.

After serving my country for 23 years in the Foreign Service, I decided it was time for a new career. Having been a passionate programmer since the age of 15, my ambition is becoming a Software Engineer. In May 2020 I started the 16 week immersive Full-Stack Web Developer boot camp at Rithm School in San Francisco to 'brush up' on my coding skills, problem solving techniques, and computer science knowledge.

In August 2020 I will start looking for my first job as a web developer. If you have any questions or suggestions, please do not hesitate to contact me.
