About

December 2019 I decided to put an end to my 24 years in the Dutch diplomatic service. At 51 years of age and living in the East Bay, I was determined to start a new career as a sofware engineer, and live happily ever after.

With the support of family and my employer, I joined the number one Software Engineer bootcamp in San Francisco in May of 2020. A 16 week immersive program centered around JavaScript, Python, PostgreSQL, Flask, Node, Express, React, Redux, and computer science fundamentals. The school's prework curriculum requires students to build a personal website, and that is how this site came to life.

I was born, raised, and educated in the Netherlands. In december 1996, I got a job offered at the Consulate General of the Netherlands in Los Angeles, got married, and moved with my wife to California. The end of 2009 the office re-located to San Francisco, so we moved too and ended up living in the East Bay. Still happily married, and proud parents of two beautiful teenage daughters.

I started programming in BASIC on my Commodore 64 when I was 15 years old. Over the years have been coding as a hobby in PHP, Ruby, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I have always loved programming for many reasons, and still do.
